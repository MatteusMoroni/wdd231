CT Patrick Ramos Fight Academy

Website Site Plan

1. Site Name

CT Patrick Ramos Fight Academy

Reason: The name uses the title "CT" (Combat Team/Coach Title) which is recognized in the martial arts world, combined with the instructor's full name to establish credibility and authority. "Fight Academy" clearly indicates the focus on professional combat training, differentiating from recreational gyms.

2. Site Purpose

The website will provide a professional digital presence for CT Patrick Ramos Fight Academy, offering:

  • Information about training programs and class schedules
  • Instructor Patrick Ramos' credentials and academy history
  • Contact details and location information
  • Visual gallery of the facility and training sessions
  • Contact form for prospective students
Primary Goal: Establish online visibility so people searching for martial arts training in the region can find, evaluate, and contact the academy through Google searches and social media references.

3. Scenarios

Scenario 1:

"What are the Muay Thai class schedules for beginners and how much does it cost?"

Scenario 2:

"What is instructor Patrick Ramos' experience and qualifications?"

Scenario 3:

"What neighborhood is the academy in and how can I contact them to schedule a trial class?"

4. Color Scheme

#C41E3A

Primary Color

Usage: Main headings, call-to-action buttons, highlight details

Reason: Represents energy, passion, and combat

#1A1A1A

Secondary Color

Usage: Main text, footer, alternating section backgrounds

Reason: Professionalism and training seriousness

#F5F5F5

Accent Color

Usage: Main backgrounds, spacing, visual contrast

Reason: Readability and visual cleanliness

5. Typography

Montserrat - Heading Font

Usage: Titles, headers, navigation

Reason: Modern, strong, and readable at different sizes

Open Sans - Body Font

This is an example of body text using Open Sans. It provides excellent readability for long texts, especially on mobile devices. The font is clean, professional, and easy on the eyes.

Usage: Body text, paragraphs, descriptions

Reason: Excellent readability for long texts, especially on mobile
